About Our Food

Falafel: Made from whole chickpeas, ground with fresh green herbs, onion and spices and then deep-fried to a golden brown perfection
Chicken Shawarma: Strips of chicken breasts marinated overnight in an exotic blend of more than a dozen spices, yogurt and citrus and then grilled to release its bold, complex flavors
Lamb Gyro: Tender ground lamb, Greek-style, grilled with Mediterranean spices
Hummus: A spread or dip made from cooked chickpeas, lemon, garlic and sesame seed paste
Tahini: A sauce made from ground sesame seeds and lemon juice
