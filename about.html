About Us

We’re bringing Nashville genuine family recipes from the Nile Valley in southern Egypt and presenting them with a restaurant craft honed in Cairo and New York City.

Because we believe you are entitled to delicious, fresh meals AND a great service experience, King Tut’s food truck in Nashville never compromises on quality. We use only the best ingredients that keep customers coming back again and again.
